Amplitude vs Frequency Stimulation

This simulation sweep seeks to understand, in-scilico*, how varying amplitude and frequency affects the activation of a cortical column (at different layers). 

This cortical column is modeled as a 400uM-by-400uM-by-2000uM column, layer depths are specified in the citation*

We stimulate each layer at this height: 

Layer1 - 125

Layer2/3 - 562 

Layer4 - 1000

Layer5 - 1437

Layer6 - 1875

We chose these depths because they are evenly spaced, enabling us to run the midpoints to double the resolution, while at the same time each of these depths falls within each of the specified layers. 

*Kumaravelu K, Sombeck J, Miller LE, Bensmaia SJ, Grill WM. (2022). Stoney vs. Histed: Quantifying the spatial effects of intracortical microstimulation Brain stimulation. 15
